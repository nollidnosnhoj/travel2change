Activity Pricing

FareHarbor handles all the activities bookings and payments. If you are hosting a free activity, then do not worry about the FareHarbor Item ID. However, if you are hosting a paid activity, then you will need to fill out the field with the FareHarbor Item ID. You will also need to insert your FareHarbor username in your host profile.

If you need help with setting up FareHarbor for your paid activity, contact us.
